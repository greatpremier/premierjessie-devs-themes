Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about scheduling service, pricing, and our guarantees.

Our emergency response team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. For critical issues like burst pipes or severe flooding, we aim to have a licensed plumber at your location within 60 minutes of your call, depending on traffic and location density. Non-critical appointments are typically scheduled within 24 hours.

We provide clear, upfront, flat-rate pricing for nearly all standard services. Once the technician diagnoses the issue, they will present a written quote for the entire job before any work begins. There are no hidden fees, and the price will not change, even if the job takes longer than expected. For large, complex installations, we provide detailed proposals.

Absolutely. Every plumber and technician on the Blue Stream team is fully licensed by the state and carries comprehensive liability insurance. We adhere to all local, state, and national plumbing codes. This ensures that your home and property are fully protected, and all work performed meets the highest standards of safety and quality.

We stand behind our work with a robust service warranty. Depending on the type of repair or installation, our labor warranty ranges from 90 days to 2 years. Furthermore, any parts or equipment we install (such as water heaters or fixtures) carry the full manufacturer's warranty, which we will handle processing for you if an issue arises.

We proudly serve the entire greater metro area and all surrounding counties. If you are unsure whether your location falls within our service area, please call our main office line at 1800-789-3210. For commercial and industrial projects, we can discuss travel arrangements for locations further afield.

Our Complete Service Range

We provide expert plumbing solutions for every situation, big or small.

Drain and Sewer Solutions

Clogged drains and sewer line problems are serious issues that require immediate, professional attention. We use advanced diagnostic tools to ensure the job is done once and done right.

High-Pressure Hydro Jetting

The most effective way to clear heavy grease, sludge, and tree roots from lines. It cleans the entire interior of the pipe, restoring full flow capacity.

  • Restores pipe capacity to near-new condition.
  • Eco-friendly, chemical-free process.
  • Recommended for restaurants and commercial kitchens.

Sewer Line Video Inspection

We insert a high-resolution camera into your sewer line to pinpoint the exact location and cause of the problem, saving time and money on unnecessary digging.

  • Precise detection of cracks, blockages, or roots.
  • Non-invasive diagnostic procedure.
  • Provides assurance before purchasing a property.

Water Quality and Hot Water Systems

Ensure your family has access to safe, clean water and reliable, energy-efficient hot water supply.

Tankless Water Heater

On-demand hot water, saving energy and space. We handle all gas and electric models.

Whole-House Filtration

Installation of carbon filters, reverse osmosis, and UV systems for optimal drinking water quality.

Water Softeners

Protect appliances and reduce soap scum by removing hard minerals from your water supply.

Leak Detection and Pipe Integrity

Even small leaks can cause significant damage. Our non-destructive methods identify and fix the source quickly.

Precision Leak Detection

We utilize thermal imaging cameras, acoustic listening devices, and tracer gas to locate even the smallest slab or wall leaks without destructive searching.

Whole-Home Re-piping

Specializing in durable PEX and Copper re-piping to replace aging, corroded galvanized or polybutylene pipes, ensuring decades of clean water flow.
